Information on the causes of stammering.

What is stuttering?

Stuttering is not by birth, stammering is by birth. 100% children stammer from birth but 99% children get cured automatically but stammering happens due to some reason, breathing plays a major role in speaking. Your breathing becomes short or your breathing gets fragmented, then you speak at a higher speed in short breaths and stammer, and when you stutter continuously, it gets stuck in your mind that I stammer, later this becomes your psychology. And you are not able to speak clearly. You are intelligent, educated, you apply your mind, adopt all the methods, speak slowly but are not able to speak properly because you do not know even 1% about it.

There are 4 reasons for short breathing-

  1. Physical weakness or any major or prolonged illness in childhood, like typhoid, asthma, jaundice etc.
  2. There is someone stammering in the family who taught you to speak or you imitated him.
  3. Because of fear, you may have been bullied a lot in your childhood.
  4. Or any major accident or mishap has happened to you. Stuttering first occurs in childhood, before the age of 10 years. Stuttering increases with age in 90% of people and decreases in 10% of people.
